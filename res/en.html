Graduated from Tokyo university of the Arts and Goldsmiths with Fine Arts, Yukino is specialized at drawing. It is her ways of seeing and capturing the world.
After worked as a graphic designer and UX designer, in London and Tokyo, she's currently work in Tokyo as a product manager. She describes her work is finding a balanced spot between human & nature, and function & emotion.
